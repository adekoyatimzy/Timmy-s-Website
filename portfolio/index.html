Photo Manipulation

This project involved creating an informative and visually engaging poster for a Praise Night event. The aim was to design an image that appealed to a wide audience — toddlers, teens, young adults and parents — while balancing fun, clarity and maturity. Alongside developing the main poster concept, I explored multiple layout ideas and refined the strongest design to ensure it communicated the event’s purpose effectively. The final poster reflects inclusivity, a spiritual atmosphere and strong visual communication, making it suitable for both digital display and printed promotion.

Website Development

This project involved designing and developing a personal portfolio website to present my creative work in a clear, organised and visually engaging way. The aim was to build a website that showcases my skills and portfolio effectively while maintaining a modern, professional appearance. Throughout the development process, I explored different design styles, colour schemes and page structures to ensure the website remained consistent, user friendly and visually balanced. I focused on creating clean layouts, strong visual hierarchy and smooth navigation to enhance the overall user experience. The final website reflects a professional digital presence with a cohesive design suitable for both academic use and future creative opportunities. I look forward to creating more websites for clients in the future as I continue to develop my skills and expand my portfolio.
